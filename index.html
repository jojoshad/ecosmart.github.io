Ecosmart

A payment mobile application for the youth!

Ecosmart offers teenagers a solution: a new way to store and use their pocket money.

The problematic: Lots of teenagers receive pocket money from their parents.

This pocket money is often given in cash form, unpractical to use nowadays.
Moreover, many young people have trouble saving their money correctly.

Ecosmart offers a solution:

A new way to store and use their pocket money. Parents could transfer these amounts to their children using this application. Meanwhile, their kids will then be able to use it more easily through mobile payment.
